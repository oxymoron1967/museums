The Museum of American Finance

Why The Museum of American Finance is great.

The Museum of American Finance is currently closed, though they are still have some programs going on.

Some favorite exhibits

  • America In Circulation: A History of US Currency Featuring the Collection of Mark R. Shenkman

    This is about currency from colonial times to the present. It had lots of examples, including currency from the times of the Articles of Confederation, when states made their own money and Confederate money from the American Civil War.

  • Worth Its Weight: Gold from the Ground Up

    This exhibit discussed the use of gold in everything from ancient coinage to a gold Monopoly board.
