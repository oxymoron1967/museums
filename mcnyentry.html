The Museum of the City of New York

Why visit the Museum of the City of New York?

The MCNY is great. It's permanent exhibit on protests in NYC is really good. It starts with the Flushing Remonstrance up to protests on LGBT rights and beyond. MCNY has a huge photo archive that they use well here, especially for the LGBT Rights and Women's Suffrage movements.

Some favorite exhibits

  • Posters and Patriotism: Selling World War I in New York

    This was a large collection of WWI posters, and they were in amazing condition, but that wasn't all there was to the exhibit. IT invluded some short ads and movies played to encourage support for the war and relief efforts.

  • Mod New York: Fashion Takes a Trip

    This exhibit focused on fashion from the early 60's: Jackie Kennedy dominated this section, up through the early 70's. It wasn't just clothes, it also included jewelry and accessories.
