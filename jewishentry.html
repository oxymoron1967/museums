The Jewish Museum

Why Visit The Jewish Museum?

The Jewish Museum's permanent collection is amazing. One of the things I learned from it is that there really isn't a standard menorah, so there are lots of varieties. The collection has things from antiquity and the JEwish diaspora to today.

Some favorite exhibits

  • Marc Camille Chaimowicz: Your Place or Mine

    Chaimowicz is an artist who works in design, sculpture, and painting. This exhibit focused on his design, including furniture and small sculptures.

  • Florine Stettheimer: Painting Poetry Florine Stettheimer was the daughter of a banker. A painter, she was the first female artist to have a solo retrospective exhibition at the Museum of Modenr Art.
  • Isaac Mizrahi: An Unruly History

    Mizrahi is a fashion designer who has been in the business since the late 80's. This retrospective included everything from drawings to fashion and costume designs.
