The Museum of Art and Design

Why visit the Museum of Art and Design?

The Museum of Art and Design focuses on the decorative arts and crafts and other forms of design. Their permanent jewelry collection is something to see. One of the things I love about MAD is that it always goes all out. Its exhibits are always worth seeing, even when they don't quote work.

  • Sonic Arcade: Shaping Space with Sound

    This exhibit included a gallery where the artist/musician built out a wall then tuned it, and strung it,so you could play it. I was the only person there when I went to see it, and spent a good twenty monutes pkaying the wall. It was so much fun.
