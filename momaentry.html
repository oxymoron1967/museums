The Museum of Modern Art

Why visit the Museum of Modern Art?

If you like contemporary art, MoMA is the place for you. It also get some of the most amazing exhibitions.

Some favorite exhibits

  • Diego Rivera: Murals for The Museum of Modern Art

    In the United States, Diego Rivera is probably best known for the mural he painted for Rockefeller Center that was later destroyed, but he is one of the best-known and most influential of the muralists, a Mexican art movement that inffluenced art in the United States. This was an amazing look at this work.

  • Tim Burton

    Burton's career has been full of unforgettable imagery. This exhibit had models and set peices from his movies and other artwork.
