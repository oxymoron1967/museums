The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Why The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum is worth visiting

The work this museum does is important and vital. Many of the survivors and witnesses ti the Holocaust are gone, but this museum, and The Museum of Jewish Heritage: A living Memorial to the Holocaust remind us of the horrors people can inflict on one another.
