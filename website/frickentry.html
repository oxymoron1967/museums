The Frick Museum

Why Visit The Frick?

The Frick family mansion in NYC is filled with art. In many ways, it's a greatest hits of Western Art. If you've ever taken an art history class, you will recognize many of the paintings. The furnishings are the same as when the family lived there. I especially love the indoor garden. It's just a great place to sit and relax.
