The Museum of Jewish Heritage: A Living Memorial to the Holocaust

Why visit The Museum of Jewish Heritage: A Living Memorial to the Holocaust?

This museum discusses the Holocaust all the way from anti-Semitism before the Holocaust up through the end of WWII. It is a somber, sobering place, but it is important to remember.

Some favorite exhibits

  • Auschwitz: Not Long Ago, Not Far Away

    Over one million people died at Auschwitz, and this exhibit discusses the camp, and what happnened there. We need to remember, especially nowasays, with hate groups and hate crimes on the rise.
